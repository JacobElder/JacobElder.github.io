I am data scientist and human behavior researcher with 7+ years of experience working with computational and statistical models of quantitative data. Passionate about designing research to understand what motivates behaviors and beliefs. I am proficient at exploring data and addressing research questions across a variety of analytic approaches and programming languages, and in teaching and communicating statistics and research to audiences and collaborators.

Research

Jacob investigates how people's beliefs about themselves and their social groups motivate behavior and cognition. Further, he investigates how people use inferred cognitive structures to generalize and make social inferences.
